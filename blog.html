Modernizing and developing the security system for the National Commercial Bank

  • Ghaleb Mansour
  • Security System
  • 2

I was assigned by my company to supervise a group of teams that includes engineers and technicians to be a part of huge project that includes updating and developing the bank's security system in terms of fire alarm, cameras, access door, card reader, all connected to one system to protect the bank from thefts and fires and also protect customers inside the bank.

Access Door / Card Reader

The system can specify the employee who are allowed to enter and exit the gates through the card assigned to the employee and it is possible to specify a specific scope for each employee to enter according to the requirements of his job and when the employee enters from one of the gates the system automatically stores the entry data from the time and date of entry and sends it to the main system to know that this employee has He entered the gate at the specified time and date.

Cameras

The network of cameras is the most important part of the project, which aims to cover every part of the site and connect it to the control room. The cameras used take data to store it in the sub-server to transfer it to the main system. The cameras used are fire-fighting, as they can help find out the location and cause of the fire.

My responsibilities in the project.

My responsibility is to create a business plan to distribute the tasks to the technicians and make them several teams. Each team is responsible for a specific part of the project. After completing the installations, I connect the main cabinet, program the network station, create the security system for the branch and connect it to the main center.
